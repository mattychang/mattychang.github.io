Intersections - Case Western Reserve University, July 2025

Maya, an undergrad in the Gryder lab, presented her research at Intersections! She shared preliminary findings about the effect IHK-44, a novel dual CBP/p300 inhibitor, has on cell proliferation in FP-RMS, FN-RMS, and non-malignant myoblasts cell lines. Future directions aim to profile IHK-44 through the Broad Institute's PRISM screen as part of the DepMap project and assess IHK-44's mechanism of cell death.
